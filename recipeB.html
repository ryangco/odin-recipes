Red Beans and Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups red beans
  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 tsp. minced garlic
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tsp. chili powder
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp. cumin
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 dash cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Saute cooked beans in 1 tablespoon or more of olive oil with 1 teaspoon minced garlic around 1 minute.
  2. Add the cooked rice to a bowl with bean mixture.
  3. Toss with all seasonings, add the olive oil to the mixture blend together.
  4. Serve warm with your favorite barbecued meat.
